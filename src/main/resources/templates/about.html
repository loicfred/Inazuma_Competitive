In the past, community members would get their clan roles and tags by asking the mods, or note down their tops in their blocknote. But everything changed when the idea of making a discord bot to handle of this came to mind. The idea was to centralise and automatise little thing which was annoying in the community. It started with the automatic clan roles, then tournament storage... and throughout the years evolved into even automatising tournament organisation.
